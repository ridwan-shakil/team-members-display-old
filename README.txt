This plugin creates a Custom Post Type that allows you to create teams and add members to those teams. This plugin also gives a settings page where you can customize team members' design. 


== Description ==

By using this plugin you can create as many teams as you want and can add members to those teams. You can add members' Image,
Name, Designation, Short description, and  Here you get the freedom to choose whatever social account you want to add from a long list of popular social media accounts.

You can easily rearrange the member's position by dragging and dropping.

You will get a settings page from where you can control the design of your teams. Even you get a section to add your own CSS so that you can design your teams as you want.

You can display your teams by using shortcode.



### Features Of Plugin

* Fully Responsive And Clean Design
* All COlors, Font size, Image size, etc. are customizable
* Add Limitless Teams And Team Members

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `team-members-display` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Now create New teams and add team members to those teams.
4. Copy your published Team shortcode and paste it into your page or post to display it.



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I Add Unlimited Team Members with the help of this team plugin? =

You are absolutely free to add as many team members as you need. Plus, you can effortlessly adjust their positions by simply dragging and dropping them into place.

= Can I showcase my teams on any post or page?  =

Of course! You can effortlessly display your teams on any page or post by using the team shortcode, like this: [TEAM_B id=xx] (replace 'xx' with your shortcode ID).

= Am I able to customize the design of my team members' output with my own CSS?  =

Certainly, you can achieve a unique design by applying your custom CSS to the frontend display.
You can add your custom CSS from the team members display plugin settings page. 

= Does it support adding social profiles for team members? =

Yes, you can easily include social profiles for your team members.
